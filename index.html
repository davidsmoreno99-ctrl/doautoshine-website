Who We Are

Passionate About Cars.
Based in Cavan.

DoAutoShine is a small, locally-based mobile car detailing team from Cavan, Ireland. We started this business because we genuinely love cars and take pride in every single detail – literally.

We're not a faceless franchise. We're your neighbours. When we show up at your door, you get a team that cares deeply about quality, treats your car like it's their own, and won't leave until the job is done right.

Being fully mobile means we fit around your schedule – whether you're at home, at work, or anywhere in between. No travel, no hassle – just a spotless car waiting for you.
